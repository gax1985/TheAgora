
# Security Policy

First and foremost: **Thank you.** We appreciate the efforts of security researchers and community members who work to keep open-source projects safe.

Because Demos is designed to amplify community voices and eventually handle civic voting data, maintaining the integrity and privacy of this platform is our highest priority.

If you discover a security vulnerability within Demos, we ask that you **please do not open a public GitHub issue** immediately. This gives us time to protect users before the flaw is made public.

**How to report:**
1.  Please email us at theagora.outing740@passmail.net  with the subject line `SECURITY: Demos Vulnerability`.
2.  Include a description of the vulnerability and steps to reproduce it.
3.  We will acknowledge your email within 48 hours.

**In Scope:**

* Privacy leaks.

**Out of Scope:**
* Spam or social engineering attacks.
* Bugs in third-party libraries (unless it's a misconfiguration on our end).
