We recognize that contributions come in many forms‚Äînot just code. We value documentation, design, research, and community management equally.

### 1. Contributors
Everyone who interacts with the project is a Contributor.
* **Who they are:** Anyone who comments on an issue, submits a Pull Request, edits the Wiki, or helps others in discussions.
* **Rights:** To have your voice heard, your contributions reviewed fairly, and to be treated with respect.

### 2. Maintainers
Maintainers are Contributors who have shown dedication and responsibility.
* **Who they are:** Active contributors who have been invited to join the organization with "Write" access.
* **Responsibilities:**
    * Triage issues and review Pull Requests.
    * Merge approved changes.
    * Ensure code quality and security standards.
    * Foster a welcoming environment.

### 3. Project Leads
Project Leads are responsible for the overall direction and health of the project.
* **Responsibilities:**
    * Setting the high-level roadmap and vision.
    * Managing project infrastructure (servers, keys, domains).
    * resolving final disputes when consensus cannot be reached.
    * Ensuring the Code of Conduct is enforced.
